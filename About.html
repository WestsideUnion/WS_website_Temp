About Us

You provide the vision. We provide the solution.

A Technology-Driven Business Solutions Provider founded in 2009, Westside Union began as a design firm specializing in branding, UI/UX, and visual storytelling. Today, we’ve evolved into a cross-functional solution provider—delivering integrated technology, design, and consulting services that help businesses solve problems, reduce costs, and operate more efficiently.

With a core focus on software, hardware, AI, and data-driven innovation, we design and build custom solutions that are practical, scalable, and beautifully crafted. From product development to process automation, our multidisciplinary team bridges creativity and technical precision to help clients succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Headquartered in Toronto and San Francisco, we leverage global time zones to work around the clock—ensuring high-quality execution with no downtime.

Whether you’re launching a new product, streamlining operations, or reimagining your customer experience, Westside Union is your partner from concept to deployment.

history

2009

Westside Union was founded as a studio by two ambitious young people, Kai Han (Jacky) Ho from Taiwan and Jerry Schuman from the United States.

Cross Asia and North America take different time zones as an advantage, perfectly doing the project with no time lag, best quality, and without wasting any time.

2013

CTR Strategy Inc. invested in Westside Union Studio and rebranded it as Westside Union Inc.

We became a division under CTR Strategy Inc., expanded our team to include VFX artists and software and hardware engineers, and started offering software, hardware development, and 3D-printing prototype services.

2014

Westside Union Inc. joined the Google Ignite program, started an R&D department, and developed our SaaS products or projects, including Web3 applications. Project BitChat and Project BizApp were the first experimental projects at the beginning.
BitChat is a military-grade encryption enterprise messager software, and BizApp is a web-based app development tool for small businesses.

2015

Our first medical-grade project, "Project Leap M (Bio Union)" started with cutting-edge smart garments to help people monitor cardiac conditions, treat progress, and prevent sudden incidents from occurring. It combines real-time ECG monitoring with a report that can be sent out to help doctors follow up and manage a patient's condition or give proper treatment instructions easily.

2016

Our founder developed the biggest interactive 3D hologram system, "Project LEIA" for Crystal Galleria in Shanghai, China.
A hologram system with a built-in motion sensor can track your finger movements to interact with the 3D hologram system. 
In the same year, we started to invest and acquire startup companies

2017

Our founder was invited to the MEDICA Connected Health Forum as a panel speaker to present his latest research and development of Project Leap M. The year after, Project Leap M separated as a company, "Bio Union" was founded in San Francisco.

*MEDICA is the world's largest event for the medical sector for more than 40 years

2018

Our founder was invited to be the founding member of Taiwan Blockchain SRO (Self-Regulatory Organization)​ due to our years of research experience in Blockchain and Cryptocurrency. Taiwan Blockchain SRO aims to help the government draft the regulation law act to create a better environment for any related startups.

2025

A whole new chapter for the Westside Union Inc.

Westside Union Corp was founded in Toronto, Canada, to expand business in Canada.
